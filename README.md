# Phoenix Intel Grid

## Operational Intelligence for Public Resilience

Phoenix Intel Grid is a dignity-first operational intelligence platform designed to detect system instability before human harm occurs.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:
Dignity Over Power.

---

# Mission

To create transparent, predictive, human-readable operational intelligence systems for civic infrastructure, environmental monitoring, and public resilience.

---

# Core Capabilities

- Real-time telemetry ingestion
- Predictive anomaly detection
- Geospatial intelligence layers
- Public transparency dashboards
- Executive operations views
- Environmental monitoring
- Incident escalation systems
- Risk visualization
- Fail-soft operational architecture

---

# System Principles

## Prevention Over Reaction
Early intervention reduces systemic collapse.

## No Silent Failures
Critical operational failures must never occur invisibly.

## Human Escalation Authority
AI augments oversight but does not replace accountability.

## Public Transparency
Operational systems should be understandable to the public.
