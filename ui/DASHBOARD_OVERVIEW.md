# Dashboard Overview

## Mission

Phoenix Intel Grid dashboards are designed to provide human-readable operational awareness during stable conditions and periods of instability.

Built under the DOP-AI doctrine:

> Dignity Over Power

The dashboard layer prioritizes:

- clarity
- transparency
- operational visibility
- escalation awareness
- public readability
- fail-soft information systems

Dashboards exist to improve understanding and coordination.

Not to overwhelm operators with unnecessary complexity.
